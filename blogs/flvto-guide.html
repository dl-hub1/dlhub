How to Use FLVTO: Convert YouTube to MP3, MP4, FLAC and More

By DLHub Team·April 8, 2026·4 min read
⚙️

FLVTO is one of the most versatile free YouTube converters available. Unlike many tools that only output MP3, FLVTO supports multiple formats including FLAC, AVI, and OGG. This guide walks you through the entire process of using FLVTO, from finding your video to choosing the right format for your needs.

Step-by-Step: Converting a YouTube Video with FLVTO

Step 1
Copy Your YouTube URL

Open YouTube, find the video you want, and copy the full URL from your browser's address bar.

Step 2
Open FLVTO

Go to flvto.biz in any web browser. The homepage shows a simple input box at the top.

Step 3
Paste the URL

Paste your YouTube link into the FLVTO input field.

Step 4
Select Your Format

Click the format dropdown (usually shows "MP3" by default) and choose your desired output format: MP3, MP4, AVI, FLAC, or OGG.

Step 5
Convert and Download

Click the Convert button. Within seconds, a Download button will appear. Click it to save the file to your device.

💡 FLAC Tip: If you want the highest possible audio quality, select FLAC instead of MP3. FLAC is a lossless format — perfect for music production, archiving, or audiophile listening. Note that FLAC files are 3–5x larger than MP3 files.

Choosing the Right Format

  • MP3 — Best for everyday music listening. Small file, plays everywhere.
  • MP4 — Best when you want the video too. Larger file but includes visuals.
  • FLAC — Best for highest audio quality. Much larger file size.
  • AVI — Old video format. Use only if your device needs it specifically.
  • OGG — Open-source audio. Good for Linux devices and open-source media players.

Platforms FLVTO Supports

FLVTO works with more than just YouTube. You can paste URLs from:

  • YouTube
  • Vimeo
  • Dailymotion
  • SoundCloud
  • Facebook Video

Just paste any video URL from these platforms and FLVTO will handle the conversion automatically.

Tips for Best Results

  • Use FLAC if audio quality is very important to you
  • Stick with MP3 if storage space is limited
  • If a conversion fails, try again — server load can cause temporary errors
  • Install an ad blocker to make the FLVTO page much cleaner
  • For very long videos (over 1 hour), expect conversion to take 1–2 minutes

Troubleshooting

Conversion fails: Try refreshing the page and repeating the process. Sometimes FLVTO servers are busy.

Download does not start: Try right-clicking the Download button and selecting "Save link as" to force the download.

File sounds wrong: Make sure you selected the right format. If you wanted audio only, check that you did not accidentally choose MP4.
