YTMP4 Complete Guide 2026: Download YouTube Videos in HD for Free

By DLHub Team·April 5, 2026·5 min read
🎬

YTMP4 is the go-to tool whenever you want to save a YouTube video — not just the audio, but the full video — directly to your device. Whether it is a tutorial you want to watch offline, a workout video, a music video, or a documentary, YTMP4 makes saving it simple and free. This guide covers everything you need to know.

What Is YTMP4?

YTMP4 is a free online YouTube video downloader. You paste a YouTube video link, choose your preferred video quality, and download the complete video as an MP4 file. The tool is web-based — no software installation needed — and works on phones, tablets, and computers.

Think of it as the video-focused sister tool to YTMP3. While YTMP3 strips out only the audio, YTMP4 preserves the full video alongside the audio so you can watch it just like on YouTube, but offline and on any device.

Step-by-Step: How to Use YTMP4

Step 1
Find Your YouTube Video

Open YouTube and navigate to the video you want to download. Copy the full URL from the browser address bar.

Step 2
Open YTMP4

Open a new tab and go to the YTMP4 website (ytmp4.cc or similar). You will see a clean input box on the homepage.

Step 3
Paste the YouTube URL

Click inside the input box and paste the YouTube URL you copied. Double-check that the full link is pasted.

Step 4
Click Convert

Press the Convert button. YTMP4 will process the video and prepare the download options. This usually takes 5–20 seconds.

Step 5
Choose Quality and Download

Select your preferred video quality (360p, 480p, 720p, or 1080p). Click the Download button and the MP4 file will save to your device.

💡 Quality Tip: For watching on a phone, 480p or 720p gives the best balance of quality and file size. For watching on a TV or large monitor, choose 1080p. For very slow connections, use 360p to download quickly.

Understanding Video Quality Options

📱 360p

Small file (~30–50 MB per 10 min). Fine for small phone screens on slow connections.

💻 480p

Medium file (~60–100 MB per 10 min). Good balance for mobile and tablet viewing.

🖥️ 720p HD

Good quality (~100–180 MB per 10 min). Recommended for most users.

🎥 1080p Full HD

Best quality (~200–400 MB per 10 min). Ideal for TV or desktop viewing.

Best Uses for YTMP4

  • Saving tutorial or how-to videos to watch without internet
  • Downloading workout videos for the gym where Wi-Fi may be unavailable
  • Keeping documentary or educational content for offline study
  • Saving music videos when you want both audio and visuals
  • Archiving videos before they get deleted or age-restricted
  • Sharing videos in areas with poor connectivity

YTMP4 Pros and Cons

✅ Pros

  • Multiple quality options up to 1080p
  • No software installation needed
  • Free to use with no account
  • Works on all devices
  • Fast conversion for short videos

❌ Cons

  • Large file sizes for HD videos
  • Ads on the download page
  • Longer conversion for long videos
  • No playlist download
  • Requires stable internet

YTMP4 on Mobile (Android & iPhone)

Android: YTMP4 works perfectly in Chrome. After clicking Download, the MP4 file goes to your Downloads folder and can be played in any video app (Google Photos, VLC, MX Player).

iPhone: Safari on iOS may not download MP4 files directly. Use Firefox for iOS instead — it handles MP4 downloads much better. After downloading, the file goes to your Files app and can be shared to Photos or any video app.

Storage Requirements

Before downloading a long video in high quality, make sure your device has enough storage. A 1-hour documentary at 1080p can be 1–2 GB. A 1-hour video at 480p is typically 300–500 MB. Check your available storage before starting large downloads to avoid incomplete files.

Final Tips

  • Use an ad blocker to avoid clicking fake download buttons by mistake
  • Choose 720p as your default — it looks great and saves storage
  • Connect to Wi-Fi before downloading large HD videos
  • After downloading, you can watch the video completely offline forever
