How to Use Tubidy: Complete Step-by-Step Guide to Download Music & Videos

By DLHub Team·April 28, 2026·5 min read
📲

Tubidy is one of the easiest ways to download free music and videos directly to your phone or computer. Whether you want to save your favorite song as an MP3 or keep a video as an MP4, Tubidy makes the entire process simple. This guide walks you through exactly how to use Tubidy — even if it is your very first time.

What You Need Before You Start

Using Tubidy requires almost nothing. Here is what you need:

  • A smartphone, tablet, or computer with a web browser
  • An internet connection (even a mobile data connection works)
  • The name of the song, artist, or video you want to download

That is it. You do not need to install any app, create any account, or pay anything. Tubidy works entirely in your browser.

Step-by-Step: How to Download MP3 Music from Tubidy

Step 1
Open Tubidy in Your Browser

Go to tubidy.io in any browser on your phone or computer. The website loads quickly even on mobile data.

Step 2
Search for Your Song

Type the song name, artist, or video title in the search box at the top of the page. Hit the search button or press Enter.

Step 3
Browse the Results

Tubidy shows a list of results matching your search. Each result has a title and a small icon. Look through the results to find the one you want.

Step 4
Choose Your Format

Click on the result you want. You will see options to download as MP3 (audio only) or MP4 (video). Choose based on your needs.

Step 5
Download the File

Tap or click the download button. Your file will start downloading immediately. On mobile, it usually goes to your Downloads folder.

💡 Pro Tip: If you see multiple download links on the page, make sure to click the one that says "Download" clearly — not the banner ads that look like download buttons. Using a browser with a built-in ad blocker (like Brave) makes this much easier.

How to Download MP4 Video from Tubidy

The process for downloading video is the same as downloading audio. When you reach the download options, simply choose "MP4" instead of "MP3." Tubidy will then provide a video file you can save and watch offline.

Keep in mind that video files are larger than audio files, so make sure you have enough storage space on your device before downloading.

Tubidy on Android vs iPhone

Tubidy works on both Android and iPhone browsers without any app needed. However, there is a small difference:

  • Android: Files download directly to your Downloads folder and can be played in any music or video player app.
  • iPhone (iOS): Safari may ask where to save the file. You can save it to your Files app. Some iPhones may need a third-party browser like Firefox to download MP3 files smoothly.

Tips for a Better Tubidy Experience

  • Use specific search terms — searching "artist name + song title" gives better results than just a song title
  • Try different spellings if your first search does not find results
  • Use an ad blocker to avoid confusing ad buttons
  • Check your device's storage before downloading large video files
  • Bookmark Tubidy for quick access next time

Common Problems and How to Fix Them

Download not starting? Try a different browser. Chrome and Firefox usually work best on both Android and desktop.

File not playing after download? Make sure you have a media player installed. VLC is a free app that plays almost any audio or video format.

Cannot find the song I want? Tubidy's library is not unlimited. If a song is not found, try MP3juice or YTMP3 as an alternative.

Page loading too slow? Tubidy is lightweight but ads can slow it down. Try disabling some browser extensions temporarily or using a faster connection.

Is Tubidy Legal to Use?

Using Tubidy for personal, non-commercial use is generally acceptable in most countries. However, downloading copyrighted music or video without permission from the rights holder may violate copyright law depending on your location. Always check the copyright status of the content you are downloading, and use Tubidy responsibly for personal enjoyment only.
