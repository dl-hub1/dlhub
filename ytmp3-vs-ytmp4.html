YTMP3 vs YTMP4: Which YouTube Downloader Should You Use in 2026?

By DLHub Team·April 20, 2026·5 min read
⚖️

If you have ever wanted to save a YouTube video or song to your device, you have probably come across both YTMP3 and YTMP4. They look similar and even share part of their name — but they serve different purposes. This guide explains the key differences so you can always choose the right tool.

The Simple Answer

The core difference is simple: YTMP3 downloads audio only (MP3), while YTMP4 downloads video with audio (MP4). If you want music or a podcast, use YTMP3. If you want to save a video to watch offline, use YTMP4.

When to Use YTMP3

Use YTMP3 when: Audio Focus

  • You want to save a song to listen offline
  • You need a small file that fits on any device
  • You are downloading podcast episodes or interviews
  • You want to add music to a video project
  • Storage space is limited on your phone

When to Use YTMP4

Use YTMP4 when: Video Focus

  • You want to watch a video offline without internet
  • You need a tutorial or how-to video saved locally
  • You are saving a music video, not just audio
  • You need to share or edit the video later
  • Quality visuals matter for your content

Quality Comparison

For audio, YTMP3 at 320 kbps sounds excellent — comparable to CD quality. Most users will not notice a difference between 128 kbps and 320 kbps on standard earphones. For video, YTMP4 can deliver up to 1080p Full HD depending on the original YouTube video quality.

If audio quality matters most to you (music production, audiophile listening), YTMP3 at the highest available bitrate is the right choice. If you need pristine video quality for professional use, YTMP4 at 1080p is the way to go.

File Size Differences

A typical 3-minute song downloaded as MP3 at 128 kbps is around 3 MB. At 320 kbps, it grows to about 7 MB. The same 3-minute video as MP4 at 720p is roughly 50–80 MB. At 1080p, it can be 100–150 MB. This is an important consideration if your device has limited storage or you have a slow internet connection.

Which Is More Popular?

YTMP3 gets more daily searches and usage because most people primarily want to download music. However, YTMP4 has grown significantly in 2025–2026 as more users want to save tutorial videos, workout clips, and educational content for offline viewing.

Our Recommendation

Use YTMP3 for 90% of your needs — it is faster, lighter, and perfect for music. Switch to YTMP4 when you specifically need the video component. Both tools are free, easy to use, and work right in your browser.
