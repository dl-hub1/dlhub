FLVTO Review 2026: YouTube to MP3 Converter Thoroughly Tested

By DLHub Team·April 10, 2026·6 min read
🔄

FLVTO has been a staple YouTube-to-MP3 converter for many years. Known for its speed and reliability, it attracted millions of users before facing legal challenges. In 2026, we revisited FLVTO to see how it performs and whether it still deserves a place in your download toolkit.

What Is FLVTO?

FLVTO (originally at flvto.biz) is a free online converter that takes a YouTube URL and converts the video into a downloadable MP3 or MP4 file. It was one of the first major YouTube-to-MP3 converters and built a large user base due to its fast conversion speeds and support for multiple output formats.

FLVTO also supports links from other platforms including Vimeo, Dailymotion, and SoundCloud, making it more versatile than tools that only work with YouTube.

How to Use FLVTO

  1. Go to flvto.biz in your browser.
  2. Open YouTube and copy the URL of the video you want to convert.
  3. Paste the URL into the FLVTO input box.
  4. Select your preferred format — MP3, MP4, AVI, or others.
  5. Click the Convert button and wait for the conversion to complete.
  6. Download the finished file to your device.

Supported Formats

FLVTO stands out from most converters by supporting more output formats than just MP3 and MP4:

  • MP3 — Standard audio format, universal compatibility
  • MP4 — Video with audio, great for offline video watching
  • AVI — Older video format, compatible with many desktop players
  • FLAC — Lossless audio for audiophiles who need highest quality
  • OGG — Open-source audio format for Linux and media players

This multi-format support is FLVTO's biggest advantage over competitors that only offer MP3 or MP4.

Conversion Speed

FLVTO is known for fast conversions. In our tests, a standard 4-minute song converted in under 10 seconds. Longer videos took proportionally more time but remained among the faster converters we tested. The speed depends partly on server load — peak hours can slow things down slightly.

FLVTO vs YTMP3

For basic MP3 downloads, YTMP3 and FLVTO perform very similarly. FLVTO wins on format variety — it is the only free tool in this category that offers FLAC lossless audio downloads. YTMP3 tends to have a slightly cleaner interface and fewer ads. If you only ever need MP3, YTMP3 is slightly better. If you sometimes need other formats, FLVTO is the stronger choice.

Is FLVTO Safe?

FLVTO itself does not install software on your device. However, the site has many ads, including some that open pop-up windows. Use an ad blocker and be careful about what you click. The conversion service itself is safe — it simply processes the audio from the video you provide.

Final Verdict

FLVTO is a reliable and feature-rich YouTube converter that earns its place in 2026, particularly for users who need format diversity. The FLAC option alone makes it unique among free tools. While the heavy advertising is a drawback, FLVTO's speed and multi-platform support make it a solid choice for power users who want more than just MP3.
